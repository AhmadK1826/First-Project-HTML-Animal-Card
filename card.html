Lion

An animal famous for its physical strength, as it has a large muscular body, a large head, and short legs

  • Average length:2.5 to 3.3 meters
  • Average lifespan:10 to 14 years.
  • Scientific name:Panthera leo

lions are social animals that live in groups called prides. Males have a distinctive mane, while females are the primary hunters. They are apex predators, relying on teamwork to hunt prey like zebras and antelopes.
